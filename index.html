Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival

5th of August - 8th of August 2019
This is a festival for those who love the comfort of lawn chairs and the open air. It's an event for people who like sharing their popcorn with the local squirrels. Bring your kids, bring your dog, bring your cat (if you're into that) and sit down for an evening of fine cinema!

We are going to show two movies every night of the Festival - at 7pm and at 10pm. Before and between the shows you will be able to get snacks and refreshments, socialise, mingle around and find your perfect seat!

The event is created and organised by Jennifer Viala, also the founder of Brooklyn Vibes Events Co - the person behind numerous stunning celebration events and special experiences around New York!

Note - attendance is free, but the number of spaces is limited. We will close registration if the amount of attendees reaches the venue’s capacity of 5,000 people. Make sure you REGISTER as soon as possible to get your tickets!

On a summer evening, there’s nothing quite like watching a movie under the stars!

NEWS

THE LATEST NEWS ABOUT THE FESTIVAL

Posted on 17.04.2019

Just a few things to remember before you come along!

* Entry and Exit will be advised. Please pay attention to the stewards instructions. They are there for your safety. There is no parking on the grounds.

* Gates will be open before the screening but the screening can’t operate before it gets dark. (That is how it is designed!)

* Our screenings are suitable for kids (8+ yrs old) too but must be accompanied by an adult. The onus is on parents to decide if a screening is suitable for their child.

* We recommend to bring blankets and warm clothes if you attend the latest screening. As you know, it may get chilly at night so make sure to wrap up!

* If you have a deck chair or camp chair, feel free to bring it with you.

* Finally, don’t forget the insect repellent! We’ll be among trees and near water so bring something to deter the midges.

* Sorry but no footballs or alcohol allowed.

* Please be good to the environment, respect your town, and take everything home or make sure you use bin containers just outside the gates. Portable toilets will be located in three locations around the area - please double-check the grounds map when you enter the gates!

*OPEN AIR CINEMA is dependent on fine weather, if inclement weather is forecast, screenings may/will be cancelled. Please keep an eye on social media, Facebook and Twitter.*
